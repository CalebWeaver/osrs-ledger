## Crafting

### Gold Jewelry (Rings, Necklaces, Bracelets, Amulets)

Crafted at a furnace from a gold bar and optional gemstone. 3 ticks per action.

- **Rings**: Gold, sapphire, emerald, ruby, diamond, dragonstone, onyx (Crafting 5–67)
- **Necklaces**: Gold, sapphire, emerald, ruby, diamond, dragonstone, onyx (Crafting 6–82)
- **Bracelets**: Gold, sapphire, emerald, ruby, diamond, dragonstone, onyx (Crafting 7–84)
- **Amulets (unstrung)**: Gold, sapphire, emerald, ruby, diamond, dragonstone, onyx, zenyte (Crafting 8–98)

### Silver Jewelry

Crafted at a furnace from a silver bar and optional semiprecious gemstone. 3 ticks per action.

- **Rings, necklaces, bracelets, amulets**: Opal, jade, topaz variants (Crafting 1–16)
- **Other**: Holy symbol (u), unholy symbol (u), silver sickle, silver bolts (unf), tiara (Crafting 16–23)

### Stringing Amulets

Ball of wool + unstrung amulet. 2 ticks per action. Gold through zenyte (Crafting 8–98).

### Leather & Dragonhide

- **Regular leather**: Gloves, boots, cowl, vambraces, body, chaps, coif (Crafting 1–38)
- **Hard leather**: Hardleather body (Crafting 28)
- **Studded**: Studded body and chaps from leather + steel studs (Crafting 41–44)
- **Dragonhide**: Green, blue, red, black — vambraces, chaps, body (Crafting 57–84)
- **Snakeskin**: Boots, vambraces, bandana, chaps, body (Crafting 45–53)

### Glass Blowing

Molten glass into beer glass, candle lantern, oil lamp, vial, fishbowl, unpowered orb, lantern lens, light orb. 3 ticks per action (Crafting 1–87).

### Gem Cutting

Uncut gems into cut gems. 3 ticks per action. Opal through zenyte (Crafting 1–89). Lower-level gems have a failure chance that scales with level.

### Battlestaves

Elemental orb + battlestaff. 2 ticks per action. Water, earth, fire, air (Crafting 54–66).

### Spinning

Wool, flax, sinew, magic roots, hair on a spinning wheel. 5 ticks per action (Crafting 1–19).

## Fletching

### Darts

Dart tips + feathers, crafted in batches of 10. 1 tick per action. Bronze through dragon (Fletching 1–95).

### Arrows

Arrowheads + headless arrows, crafted in batches of 15. 1 tick per action. Bronze through dragon (Fletching 1–90).

### Gem-Tipped Bolts

Bolt tips + metal bolts, crafted in batches of 10. 2 ticks per action. Opal through onyx, with both standard and dragon bolt variants (Fletching 11–84).

### Bows

- **Unstrung shortbows**: Logs carved with a knife, 5 ticks per action (Fletching 5–80)
- **Unstrung longbows**: Logs carved with a knife, 7 ticks per action (Fletching 10–85)
- **Stringing shortbows**: Unstrung bow + bowstring, 2 ticks per action (Fletching 5–80)
- **Stringing longbows**: Unstrung bow + bowstring, 2 ticks per action (Fletching 10–85)

Wood types: Normal, oak, willow, maple, yew, magic.

## Magic

### Enchantment

Enchant jewelry using cosmic runes + elemental runes. 4 ticks per action.

- **Lvl-1**: Opal and sapphire jewelry (Magic 7)
- **Lvl-2**: Jade and emerald jewelry (Magic 27)
- **Lvl-3**: Topaz and ruby jewelry (Magic 49)
- **Lvl-4**: Diamond jewelry (Magic 57)
- **Lvl-5**: Dragonstone jewelry (Magic 68)
- **Lvl-6**: Onyx jewelry (Magic 87)

Each tier enchants rings, necklaces, amulets, and bracelets.

### Superheat Item

Nature rune + ore to produce a bar without a furnace. 5 ticks per action. Iron through gold (Magic 43+). Grants both Magic and Smithing XP.

### Lunar Spells

Require Lunar Diplomacy and Dream Mentor quests.

- **Plank Make**: Converts logs to planks using nature + astral runes + coins. 4 ticks. Normal, oak, teak, mahogany (Magic 86).
- **Tan Leather**: Tans 5 hides per cast. Green, blue, red, black dragonhide (Magic 78). 5 ticks.
- **Spin Flax**: Spins 5 flax into bowstrings per cast (Magic 76). 3 ticks.
- **Superglass Make**: Converts sand + seaweed/giant seaweed into molten glass with bonus yield (Magic 77). 3 ticks.
- **String Jewelry**: Strings amulets without ball of wool. 3 ticks. All gem types (Magic 80).

### Arceuus Spells

- **Degrime**: Cleans 27 grimy herbs per cast. Ranarr through dwarf weed (Magic 70). 3 ticks. Requires A Kingdom Divided.

### Teleport Tablets

Soft clay + runes at a lectern. 4 ticks per action + 1.45 ticks bank time.

- **Standard**: Varrock, Lumbridge, Falador, Camelot, Ardougne, Watchtower, House, Trollheim, Boat (Magic 25–67)
- **Ancient**: Paddewwa through Ghorrock (Magic 54–96). Requires Desert Treasure I.
- **Lunar**: Moonclan through Ice Plateau (Magic 69–89). Requires Lunar Diplomacy.
- **Arceuus**: Library through Ape Atoll (Magic 6–90). Requires Client of Kourend.

## Herblore

### Herb Cleaning

Grimy herb to clean herb. 2 ticks per action. Guam through torstol (Herblore 3–75).

### Unfinished Potions

Clean herb + vial of water. 2 ticks per action. Guam through torstol (Herblore 3–75). No XP.

### Finished Potions

Unfinished potion + secondary ingredient. 2 ticks per action.

Attack, antipoison, strength, restore, energy, defence, prayer, super attack, superantipoison, super energy, super strength, super restore, super defence, ranging, magic, saradomin brew (Herblore 3–81).

## Smithing

### Bar Smelting

Ore + coal at a furnace. 4 ticks per action. Bronze through runite (Smithing 1–85).

### Cannonballs

Steel bar into 4 cannonballs. 6 ticks per action. Requires Dwarf Cannon quest (Smithing 35).

### Platebodies

5 metal bars into a platebody at an anvil. 4 ticks per action. Bronze through adamant (Smithing 18–88).

### Knives

1 metal bar into 5 knives. 4 ticks per action. Iron through rune (Smithing 22–92).

### Dart Tips

1 metal bar into 10 dart tips. 4 ticks per action. Iron through rune (Smithing 19–89).

## Blast Furnace

Smelting bars with halved coal requirements. 3 ticks per action + 1 tick bank time. Bronze through runite. Requires The Giant Dwarf quest. ~72k gp/hr coffer cost.

## Cooking

Cook raw fish/meat on a range. 4 ticks per action. Success rate scales with Cooking level — higher levels burn less.

Shrimps, chicken, sardine, herring, mackerel, trout, salmon, tuna, lobster, swordfish, monkfish, karambwan, shark, sea turtle, anglerfish, dark crab, and more (Cooking 1–90).

## Runecraft

Craft runes from pure essence at altars. 4 ticks per action.

Air, mind, water, earth, fire, body, cosmic, chaos, nature, law, death, blood, soul, wrath (Runecraft 1–95).

Higher Runecraft levels unlock multiple runes per essence for lower-tier runes.

## Fishing

Catch fish at fishing spots. No inputs required (some methods use feathers as bait). Tick rates reflect realistic catches/hr including idle time.

Shrimps, trout, salmon, lobster, swordfish, monkfish, karambwan, shark, anglerfish, dark crab (Fishing 1–85).

Some methods are quest-gated: monkfish (Swan Song), karambwan (Tai Bwo Wannai Trio).

## Hunter

Box trapping chinchompas. No inputs required.

- **Grey chinchompas**: Hunter 53, 35 ticks per catch
- **Red chinchompas**: Hunter 63, 25 ticks per catch
- **Black chinchompas**: Hunter 73, 20 ticks per catch (Wilderness)

## Mining

Gathering ores from rocks. No inputs required. Tick rates include idle time between swings.

- **Clay**: Mining 1, ~8 ticks
- **Copper ore**: Mining 1, ~8 ticks
- **Tin ore**: Mining 1, ~8 ticks
- **Iron ore**: Mining 15, ~6 ticks
- **Silver ore**: Mining 20, ~12 ticks
- **Coal**: Mining 30, ~12 ticks
- **Gold ore**: Mining 40, ~12 ticks
- **Gem rocks**: Mining 40, ~12 ticks (outputs modeled as uncut sapphires)
- **Mithril ore**: Mining 55, ~16 ticks
- **Adamantite ore**: Mining 70, ~24 ticks
- **Runite ore**: Mining 85, ~40 ticks
- **Amethyst**: Mining 92, ~36 ticks

## Woodcutting

Chopping trees for logs. No inputs required. Tick rates include idle time between chops.

- **Regular logs**: Woodcutting 1, ~8 ticks
- **Oak logs**: Woodcutting 15, ~8 ticks
- **Willow logs**: Woodcutting 30, ~8 ticks
- **Maple logs**: Woodcutting 45, ~12 ticks
- **Yew logs**: Woodcutting 60, ~20 ticks
- **Magic logs**: Woodcutting 75, ~40 ticks
- **Redwood logs**: Woodcutting 90, ~24 ticks
